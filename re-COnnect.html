Back to Normal

To get back to normal, people should practice safety measures. Starting off with social distancing is great because this is the most basic action that can be done to minimize the risk of spreading the virus, keeping 6 feet away from someone in any circumstance is a good way to prevent the spread of the virus. Always wearing a mask is also one that can minimize the risk of spreading the virus. Lastly, take the vaccination for Covid-19, it will help every get back to normal quicker.
